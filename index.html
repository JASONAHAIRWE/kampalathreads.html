Our Story

Born in a Classroom,
Worn Across Uganda

Kampala Threads was founded in 2026 as a collaborative school project by 10 students who believed that our local fashion industry deserved a modern, digital home.

What started as an academic challenge to empower homegrown talent has evolved into a platform dedicated to connecting the tailors of Nakasero Market with customers throughout every corner of the country.

Our mission is focused and clear: celebrate Ugandan craftsmanship, support our local artisans, and demonstrate how student-led innovation can make authentic, high-quality fashion accessible to every Ugandan.

Shipping Policy

Everything you need to know about how we deliver your order.

🚚 Delivery Coverage

We deliver across Uganda including Kampala, Entebbe, Jinja, Mbarara, Gulu, Fort Portal, and over 50 more towns. We are expanding internationally — stay tuned!

⏰ Processing Time

All orders are processed within 24 hours of being placed. You will receive a confirmation notification once your package is dispatched.

💰 Shipping Costs

  • Kampala City: UGX 5,000 (free for orders above UGX 150,000)
  • Greater Kampala: UGX 10,000
  • Upcountry: UGX 15,000 – 25,000 depending on location

📦 Estimated Delivery Times

  • Kampala: 1 – 2 business days
  • Greater Kampala: 2 – 3 business days
  • Upcountry: 3 – 5 business days

Have questions about your delivery? Contact our team — we're happy to help.

Returns & Refunds

Your satisfaction is our priority. Here's our hassle-free return promise.

🔄 7-Day Return Window

If you are not fully satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 7 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging.

📝 How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact us via our contact form or call +256 701 234 567.
  2. Share your order details and reason for the return.
  3. We'll arrange a pickup or provide drop-off instructions.
  4. Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed within 3 business days.

🚫 Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom-made / tailored-to-order garments
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Items returned after the 7-day window

💰 Refund Method

Refunds are issued via Mobile Money (MTN or Airtel) or bank transfer, using the same payment method as the original order.

Need help with a return? Reach out to us — we respond within 24 hours.

Size Guide

Find the right fit with our comprehensive measurement guide.

📏 How to Measure

Use a soft measuring tape. Measure close to the body but not tight. All measurements are in centimetres (cm).

  • Bust / Chest: Measure around the fullest part of the chest.
  • Waist: Measure around the narrowest part of the waist.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of the hips.
