Our Story

Born in a Classroom,
Worn Across Uganda

Kampala Threads was founded in 2026 as a collaborative school project by 10 students who believed that our local fashion industry deserved a modern, digital home.

What started as an academic challenge to empower homegrown talent has evolved into a platform dedicated to connecting the tailors of Nakasero Market with customers throughout every corner of the country.

Our mission is focused and clear: celebrate Ugandan craftsmanship, support our local artisans, and demonstrate how student-led innovation can make authentic, high-quality fashion accessible to every Ugandan.

🧵
What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🏆 Quality First

Every garment undergoes strict quality checks before it reaches you. We partner only with skilled artisans who take pride in their craft.

🤝 Community Driven

We are committed to uplifting Ugandan artisans, designers, and fabric traders. Every purchase supports a local family and their livelihood.

🌿 Sustainable Fashion

We champion eco-friendly production methods, using natural dyes and fabrics that are kind to Uganda's beautiful natural environment.
